
## Ingredients
Broth Options (pick 1 or mix):
6 cups (1.5L) chicken/vegetable broth (store-bought or homemade with ginger, garlic, and scallions)
OR 6 cups (1.5L) spicy miso broth (add 2 tbsp miso paste + 1 tsp chili oil to base broth)
Protein:
200g (7 oz) thinly sliced beef/pork
150g (5 oz) shrimp (peeled, deveined)
100g (3.5 oz) fish balls/tofu puffs
Vegetables:
1 bok choy (chopped)
1 carrot (thinly sliced)
100g (3.5 oz) mushrooms (shiitake/enoki)
1 cup (150g) bean sprouts
Starch:
1 pack udon noodles or rice noodles
Dipping Sauce:
¼ cup soy sauce
2 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tsp Chili flakes (optional, minced garlic
for heat)
Seasoning:
Salt & white pepper (to taste)
1 tsp sesame seeds (for garnish)

## Instructions
Prep Ingredients
Slice meats/shrimp thinly; wash and chop vegetables.
Soak noodles in warm water (if dry) until soft.
Simmer Broth
Pour broth into a hotpot pot (or large saucepan). Bring to a boil, then reduce to a gentle simmer.
Add salt/pepper to taste—adjust spice level with chili oil if using.
Make Dipping Sauce
Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, and garlic in small bowls. Add chili flakes or sesame seeds for extra flavor.
Cook & Assemble
Cooking Order (cook in batches):
Seafood/Meat: Shrimp (30 sec), beef slices (10 sec) until cooked through.
Vegetables: Bok choy, mushrooms (2–3 min until tender).
Starch: Noodles (cook 3–5 min, then drain).
Dip cooked ingredients into your sauce and enjoy immediately!
Serve
Keep the broth simmering throughout the meal so everyone can cook their favorite items.
Garnish with sesame seeds or fresh cilantro if desired.
