Pudding:
##Ingredients
1/2 cup sugar 
2 tbsp water 

Custard: 
2 eggs 
2 egg yolks 
1/2 cup sugar 
2 cups milk 
1 tsp vanilla 
##Instructions
Make caramel: cook sugar and water until amber. Pour into ramekins. 
Make custard: whisk eggs, yolks, sugar. Heat milk until steaming, slowly whisk into eggs. Stir in vanilla. Pour over caramel. 
Bake: place ramekins in water bath. 325°F for 35-40 min until set but slightly jiggly. 
Cool, then refrigerate 4 hours. To serve, run knife around edge and invert.
